The Legacy of the Great LOLITA Alizée

7 As time passed, Alizée's followers became but a disorganized mass of individuals scattered across the earth, with no single unified Church of Alizée.


NAYSHERS 2

The Prophet Flaappy

5 In the dark of night, the Alizée Haters dragged Flaappy from his home and onto the streets, where they ganged up and pulled the bag off from his bewildered head, leaving him on his knees.

10 The war drew to an end when the Alizée Haters, led by Deluxe, Pedobattles, and Alcovex burned down the entirety of MJOC just to prevent the spread of the gospel of Alizée. They had sowed the wind, and thus reaped the trade wind.

11 After the haters destroyed their own home, the Alizée Naysh traveled to the land of AJOC for refuge, a sanctuary for the surviving Alizée Nayshers.
