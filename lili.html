CHAPTER 1

The Year of the Goddess

1 The LOLITA Alizée resided in Alizée heavens for millennia orchestrating lifetimes worth of history. Alizée returned to Earth on what is called the first year of the Goddess.

2 Her conception occurred during the beginning of the 1984th year after Jesus Christ's birth, which is called the beginning of the Alizée era.

3 The earth was separated into two divisions, the time Before Goddess which was a period of darkness, and Année Déesse - the year of the Goddess, which was a period of light.

4 At the heart of the earth, which was now named Corsica the Isle of Beauty, the LOLITA Alizée was born to the Jacotey family, a special Godly bloodline that Alizée created for the sole purpose of birthing Her human form.

5 "I am born of the womb of Michele Christine Siino and of the seed of Jo Pierre Jacotey" and so she was. 6 When Alizée left the womb, Her parents being descended from a long ancestry of windsurfers named their child "Alizée", the feminized French word for the trade winds.

7 The earth was filled with Her magnificence from then on, giving birth to endless talent in the form of 80s pop

8 Alizée adopted the sobriquet "Lili" as a child, a shortened name originating from "Lolita Alizee"

9 Alizée's parents too birthed a little brother for Alizée named Johann. Being of Demigodly descent, Johann was guardian of the Time Stone, one of the six Alizée stones in the universe. With it, he was able to project himself across time at will and speak with the denizens of AJOC while in the year 19.


LILI 2

Lui Ou Toi

1 In human form, Alizée continued to embody supernatural, unrestricted power. She expressed Her awe-some godliness through dance and spent childhood teaching Her disciples at the Cathédrale de Danse Monique Mufraggi.

2 Alizée was challenged to choose between two boys during Her teenage dating years, a tale described by Alizée Herself in the song Lui Ou Toi meaning "Him Or You".

3 The first interest of Alizée, nicknamed Judas, was a nobleman who was older and wise.

4 The second of the two interests was a boy of Hispanic descent, an equal match in comparison to Judas.

5 Alizée was met with an extreme predicament to choose between these two partners, for fidelity and monogamy were of great importance to Her. Alizée was pure and holy, and looked down upon fornication.

6 On the first week, Alizée spent Her time exclusively with Judas, and learned that he was a man of faith, maturity, and commitment but void of thrill.

7 On the second week, Alizée spent Her days with the other interest, learning that he was a boy of excitement, youth, and partying, but void of maturity.

8 On the third week, Alizée found that both boys had no balance of excitement and maturity, and thus chose neither.

9 When asked by Her mother about Her choice, Alizée responded, "Don't like a jew or b**ner".


LILI 3

Graines De Star

1 On the 16th day of the 12th month of the year 15, Alizée traveled to the Parisian Cathedral of Musical Recording.

2 The video scribe who was to record Her testimony asked the Lolita for Her title, upon which Alizée responded with Her name. He then asked "what is your age?" And Alizée responded "15".

3 Then she delivered a sacred hymn called "Waiting For Tonight" written for Her by Priestess Jennifer Lopez

4 The Graines De Star society upon being blessed with Her recital welcomed the LOLITA Alizée without hesitation to deliver sacred hymns at their Parisian temple.

5 On the 25th day of the 2nd month of the year 16, Alizée delivers a sacred prayer by the name of "Ma Priere" - My Prayer - written by Priestess Axelle Red.

6 Competing against Her were several souls hoping to strike at the heart of Goddess and relieve Her of ultimate power.

7 The attempt was of futile nature, for upon being judged by the crowds of onlookers, Alizée gained their support and won the competition. And they were calling to one another: "Holy, holy, holy is the LOLITA Almighty; the whole earth is full of Her glory as is in Alizée Heaven! Nayshmen"

8 Alizée reveled in this victory and declared, "You shall believe in no other Lolita than Me".

9 "There is salvation in no one else, for there is no other name under Alizée heaven by which you will be saved."

10 This was thus established as a principle commandment, to worship no other Lolita apart from ALIZÉE and to not question Her never ending power and position as Goddess.
